See yesterday's post for a description of the BubbleGrid.

The grid has been expanded a bit today, mostly on the back end. Creighton played themselves into the grid with a horrible Arch Madness loss to Illinois State. While the model still has Creighton in, and the committee claims not to consider victory margins, it could leave a bad enough taste to cut the Bluejays from the field. Ironically, Illinois State was one of our two selection misses last year after a similarly horrible loss in the MVC finals. With the model's difficulty of handling mid-majors with solid but not obvious profiles, I wouldn't be surprised to see Creighton as a miss next week. With 26 wins, they pass the smell test, but only 3-2 against the RPI Top 50 and their best win against #23 Dayton may keep them out.
